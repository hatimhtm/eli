# What's New in Eli

Plain-English notes for every update. The newest is on top, and these same notes
appear in the app when you install an update.

## 0.1.0 — the first version

Welcome to Eli! Here's what it does:

- **Write calmly.** A clean, clutter-free window made just for writing your book.
- **Stay organized.** Keep your chapters (and scenes, if you want them) in a simple sidebar — no files to find, Eli keeps everything for you.
- **Translate as you go.** Turn your writing into English one chapter at a time, with you always making the final edit.
- **Make it cozy.** Pick from many relaxing color themes and a few comfortable fonts.
- **Never lose your work.** Eli saves and backs up your book automatically.
- **Share your book.** Export it as a PDF, an e-book (EPUB), or a Word document.
- **One-click updates.** When a new version is ready, just click "Check for Updates."
